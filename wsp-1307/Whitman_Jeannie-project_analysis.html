Client: Scenic Boat Tours

Project: Website Redesign

Designer: Jeannie Whitman

Document: Project Analysis

Site Intent

This site currently consists of a landing page with a link to the single page site. The information is unorganized, with minimal styling. While the site provides the information most consumers would expect to find, it offers little reason for the consumer to either stay longer or return in the future.

The clients intent for the redesign is to have a more professional site that includes increased content, user friendly interactivity, multi device responsiveness, and search engine optimization in order to increase the clients market share in both the local and visiting tourism markets.

In order to meet the clients expectations, I will completely redesign the site. This will be accomplished by eliminating the landing page, replacing it with a home page and navigation to additional pages to house new and existing content. Using appropriate html and css coding to provide for responsiveness, and recommendations for SEO. The clients current logo will be maintained.

Competitive Analysis

The sites 3 main competitors are:

  1. blackhammockairboatrides.com
  2. sjrivercruises.com
  3. Aquatic Wonders Boat Tours

Demographics information obtained through research using Quantcast shows that the typical user/consumer for this type of site is either male or female, multi ethnic individuals in the age range of 35-65+ years old with no children having no to advanced education and a household income from 0-100k/yr.

Associated Keywords

Nature tours, boat rides, day trips, sightseeing cruises, flora and fauna, florida nature, central florida tours, adventure tours, eco tours, bird watching, wildlife, theme park alternatives, sunset cruises, springs, lakes, river tours.

Targeted Audience

Currently the sites targeted audience is tourists visiting the Orlando area. Through the use of keywords, links to and from area attractions, local merchants, and transportation options it will be possible to build on the out of town visitor as well as local visitors. The following 2 use cases identify consumers on both ends of this spectrum.

Out of Town Visitors

Marisa is a 35 year old hispanic female with a BS in biology, she will be in the Orlando area for a convention. Marisa is interested in seeing Florida's diverse wildlife and ecology.

Local Visitors

John has lived in the Orlando area for the last 10 years. He is a 57 year old white male who has recently become interested in photography and is seeking opportunities to hone his skills. Since he no longer drives, he is looking for local venues that are accessible via public transportation and offer other opportunities such as area history and restaurants so that he can make a day of his adventure.

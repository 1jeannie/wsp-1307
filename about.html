Located on Lake Osceola in Orange county Florida, just 10 minutes from Downtown Orlando, is the historic town of Winter Park. Scenic Boat Tours is proud to have been in business since 1938, providing guided cruises through the chain of lakes and canals, providing visitors and locals a rare glimpse of Central Florida's diverse flora and fauna.

Our experienced skippers and staff are here every day of the year, except Christmas day. We look forward to making your visit an enjoyable one!

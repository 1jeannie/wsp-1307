Welcome to Downtown Winter Park

Bordered by a central park that plays host to concerts and art festivals, Park Avenue is home to one-of-a-kind boutiques and well-known shops, sidewalk cafes and fine restaurants, as well as world renowned art collections in the area museums.

Downtown Winter Park's charm is magnified in truly unique details: hidden gardens and side streets that are home to unique stores, a Farmers' Market that attracts locals and visitors alike each Saturday morning, festive parades that mark the season and the Scenic Boat Tour that cruises the canals between the city's lakes overlooking the area's most historic and prestigious homes. Often called "Little Europe", Park Avenue in downtown Winter Park is a year-round destination that is sure to charm visitors of all ages.
